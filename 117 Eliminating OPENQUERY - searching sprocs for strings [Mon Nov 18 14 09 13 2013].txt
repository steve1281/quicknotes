To determine which sprocs actually use openquery:

SELECT *
	FROM sys.sql_modules m 
	INNER JOIN sys.objects o 
	ON m.object_id=o.object_id
	WHERE m.definition Like '%openquery%'

As an aside, the collation by default is:
	select databasepropertyex('DNN_WWW', 'collation') sqlcollation;
	--> SQL_Latin1_General_CP1_CI_AS  (case inssensitve)
