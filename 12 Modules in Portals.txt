Post this into your host->sql window and it will give you a summary.  It shows the modules and how many tabs they are on.  It doesn't show modules that aren't used anywhere.



select p.PortalId, dtm.FriendlyName, count (distinct t.TabId) as UsedOnTabsCount
from {databaseOwner}{objectQualifier}portals p
inner join {databaseOwner}{objectQualifier}Tabs t
 on p.PortalId = t.POrtalId
inner join {databaseOwner}{objectQualifier}TabModules tm
 on tm.TabId = t.TabId
inner join {databaseOwner}{objectQualifier}Modules m
 on m.ModuleId = tm.ModuleId
inner join {databaseOwner}{objectQualifier}ModuleDefinitions dm
 on dm.ModuleDefId = m.ModuleDefId
inner join {databaseOwner}{objectQualifier}DesktopModules dtm
 on dtm.DesktopModuleId = dm.DesktopModuleId
group by p.PortalId, dtm.FriendlyName
order by 1,2
