== Center a modal popup on another div ==

Assume the popup is id="pnlReceipt" and you want to center it on div with id="ph_parent"
Code is:

        $('#pnlReceipt').dialog('open')
        .parent() //remember .dialog() wraps the content in another <div>
        .position({ my: 'center', at: 'center', of: '#ph_parent' });
